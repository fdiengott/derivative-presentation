Movement 2 · The staircase

It wins in both seasons — minus the premium.

An option is insurance.

A premium for protection

Same two seasons as the forward, one at a time. In the crash, the spot sinks well below the strike and the floor holds — they're protected exactly like the forward, just a premium lower (the small gap below the strike). In the soar, their outcome lifts off and rides the market up; at harvest they give back only the premium (the little gap between the spot and their line). So it wins both ways. Name the rhyme out loud: an option is insurance — a premium for protection — which sets up the CDS later.
