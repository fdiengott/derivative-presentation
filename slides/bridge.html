From a moment to a stream

Some risk doesn't arrive just once.

The farmer's whole world was one harvest — a single moment.

But some risk returns — every month, every payment.

Meet the baker: a loan payment, due every single month.

Their risk isn't a price to bet on — it's a stream to manage.

This is the pivot. Recap the farmer: their whole world was one transaction, one harvest — a single moment, which is why a chart with one outcome could hold it. But some risk doesn't arrive once; it returns every month, every payment. The farmer can't show that, so we hand off to a new protagonist — the baker, who owes a loan payment every month. Notice the picture changed on its own: the charts are gone, and we're following a flow of arrows instead. We've left "betting on a price" for "trading a stream." Don't announce it — let it land.
