Movement 2 · The staircase

The option: the right, not the obligation.

Below the strike a floor protects him; above it, he keeps the upside.

The premium is the cost of that freedom — the whole line sits a little lower.

He pays a premium for the right, not the obligation, to sell at the strike. Below the strike the floor holds, just like the forward; above it the line lifts off and follows the market up. The cage has opened. The only price is the premium — the whole line sits slightly lower than the forward's.
